Hi, I'm Hideo Kojima.

Video game designer, director, producer and writer.

In 2005, Kojima founded Kojima Productions,a software house controlled by Konami, and by 2011, he was appointed vice president of Konami Digital Entertainment.

Final Konami projects and departure

In mid-2012 and in the following years after Kojima finished work on the Fox Engine, Kojima has been connected to the Silent Hill series.

During this time, he indicated that he was interested in making a Silent Hill game and the first instance of this was on August 18, 2012.
